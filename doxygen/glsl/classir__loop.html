int ir_loop::cmp

Comparison operation in the loop terminator.

If any of the loop control fields are non-NULL, this field must be one of ir_binop_less, ir_binop_greater, ir_binop_lequal, ir_binop_gequal, ir_binop_equal, or ir_binop_nequal.
