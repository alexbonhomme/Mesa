Detailed Description

This attempts to flatten if-statements to conditional assignments for GPUs with limited or no flow control support.

It can't handle other control flow being inside of its block, such as calls or loops. Hopefully loop unrolling and inlining will take care of those.

Drivers for GPUs with no control flow support should simply call

lower_if_to_cond_assign(instructions)

to attempt to flatten all if-statements.

Some GPUs (such as i965 prior to gen6) do support control flow, but have a maximum nesting depth N. Drivers for such hardware can call

lower_if_to_cond_assign(instructions, N)

to attempt to flatten any if-statements appearing at depth > N.
